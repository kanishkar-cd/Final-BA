Business Requirement Document: User Management System

1. Introduction
The system shall provide a secure user authentication mechanism.

2. Functional Requirements
FR-1: User Registration
The system shall allow users to register using their email address, password, and full name.

FR-2: User Login
The system shall allow users to log in with their email address and password.

3. Non-Functional Requirements
NFR-1: Security
Passwords shall be encrypted before storing them in the database.
